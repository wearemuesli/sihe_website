SIMPLE, NOT EASY!

SIHEYUAN is a single and local co-op action-puzzle game for 1-4 players of any age, inspired by Chinese traditional architecture (“siheyuan” = “courtyard”) and color theory.

Out soon on

A MINIMALISTIC EX-TILE-VAGANZA!

Move the tiles! Pass the tiles! Place the tiles in the matching color slots of the central grid(s)! Don’t pile up the tiles! Love the tiles as they unlock 13 different levels! Hate the tiles as they behave erratically due to 13 special modifiers! Play with the tiles in single or multiplayer mode (up to 4P)!

(Or, if you don’t like videogames, just watch the tiles falling: it’s quite an experience nonetheless!)
